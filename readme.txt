== Description ==

This Plugin is used for showing all the HelloLeads CRM form into your wordpress and make the shortcode of that form so that you can use that shortcode anywhere in to you wordpress page. You just need to configure your setting via entering your email and token provided in HelloLeads account.


== 3rd Party HelloLeads ==

This plugin will get all the CRM form in to your wordpress from HelloLeads CRM. All you need to create an account on HelloLeads and you will get all the credentials that this plugin is need to communicate with HelloLeads CRM API. == Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How To Connect HelloLeads CRM Form =

Just enter your email and token key and click on connect button.

= Where Is My HelloLeads Form Shortcode =

 You just need to visit the HLS CRM Form List menu and you will see all the form created into Helloleads CRM with their shortcode click on the copy icon for copying the shortcode and use anywhere into your page or post.
